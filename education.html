Education Committee

About

The Education Committee raises awareness surrounding neurological disorders and treatments. Members of the Education Committee lead bi-weekly general meetings, educating the organization on a variety of neuro-related topics. They come up with case studies, presentations, and interactive activities. Additionally, the Education Committee integrates updated research, guest speakers, and medical school advice into these meetings. Members also have the opportunity to produce podcasts, create infographics, or any creative outlook to educate their peers on neurodegenerative diseases. The Education Committee is also responsible for implementing the annual Neurodegenerative Disorders Awareness Night (NDAN), which is held during Spring Quarter.
